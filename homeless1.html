About Homeless Link

We are the national membership charity for organizations working directly with people who become homeless in Pakistan. We work to make services better and campaign for policy change that will help end homelessness.

Our social enterprise supports the homelessness sector through specialised software, engaging training, expert consultancy, and impactful events that also helps to fund our wider work to end homelessness for good.
